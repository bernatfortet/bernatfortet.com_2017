CalMeet
Fast 1-on1 Scheduling
Design & Dev - 2017

CalMeet is a very simple web app that aims to simplify on-demand 1-on-1 scheduling.

This is a personal project, and I worked on every aspect of it. I challenged myself to deepen my understanding of React and build, from scratch, a complete product. Again, as with the Foxie project. Being able to be part of the development allowed design to fourish to a whole new level because of the immediate communication between the two processes.
