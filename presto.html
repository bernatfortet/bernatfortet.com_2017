Presto
NBA Messaging Bot
Design - 2016

Presto is a messaging bot platform. The first bot is a buddy who knows a ton about the NBA.

I leveraged my expertise in the cards platform to provide users with bite-sized information in the form of cards. I also worked on tuning the Ai to have a friendly human personality.

Our audience was teens. Our primary use case was offering video highlights, memes and game stats.
